We federalize the way you share art with the world, because we believe that this is needed.

When will Art.ax be published?

While the ART.AX feed is already released, we are working on the actual implementation of ART.AX as a standalone system. We cannot give an exact date when ART.AX will be widely available. But you are welcome to register for the closed beta.

Why we build ART.AX

We believe there is a fundamental problem with the way artists share their work. As artists ourselves, we see every day how the climate is deteriorating for artists. While the established art platforms on the internet are supposed to be on the side of artists and people, lately we see most of these platforms adopting an AI-first policy at the expense of artists' rights. We believe there is a need to shift the paradigm and give artists a federalized way to share their art, connect and exchange in a safe and self-determined environment.

What is important to us

NoAI-"art"

We have a clear stance against AI as an independent art based on illegally and morally questionable acquired data sets.

Copyright First Policy

We do not require you to grant us any usage or transfer rights. Through our federal structure, you are solely responsible for your images, and we do not host them for you.

No invisible algorithms

We do not analyze you personally or the sum of all posts. We list art based solely on their upload date or popularity, depending on how you want your feed to look.

Customize your feed

By default, we show you the latest art in the feed in a demographic way, but you can create your own algorithms that decide by which criteria you or others see art.

Your handle, your website

If you like, you can use our @art.ax domain as your Bluesky Handle and as your Personal Portfolio Website. We will make your art visible for the whole world.

It's free forever

Thanks to our federal approach and the associated paradigm shift, we are able to realize this project without gigantic server capacities, which allows us to keep the costs minimal. If you still want to support our work, you are welcome to do so by donating.

We are currently running a closed beta, you are welcome to participate.

Please be patient as we gradually expand our beta.
